Mild Alzheimers (stage one)

Alzheimers first stage is knows as Early stage Alzheimers or mild. At this stage a person may still function independently. However, the person may begin to feel like they are having memory lapses, forgetting a word or where everyday possesions were placed. At this stage symtoms are not very noticable. Family and friends will most likely begin to notice and a doctor could identify the symptoms using certain diagnostic tools.



The symptoms at this stage include:

Coming up with the right word choice or name to express what you want to say
Remembering the names of new people
Difficulty carrying out certain tasks in social situations/ work settings
Forgetting material just read
Losing / misplacing valuable objects
Increased difficulty in organizing and planning ahead
