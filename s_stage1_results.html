Prodomal stage (stage one)

The prodomal stage of Schizophrenia occurs before phsycotic symptoms appear. During this stage a person begins to experience behavioral and thinking changes eventually progress to phsychosis. The early symptoms that occur in the prodomal stage of Schizophrenia usually involve non specific symtoms that also occur in other mental illness, like depression. For this reason indentifying people in the Preodomal stage of Schizophrenia is difficult.

Symptoms in the prodomal stage of Schizophrenia most commonly include:

Social isolation
Lack of motivation
Anxiety
Quick temper
Difficulty concentrating
Changes to ones normal routine
Sleep problems
Lack of hygiene Erratic behavior
Mild or poorly formed hallucinations
